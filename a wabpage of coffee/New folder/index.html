Welcome to Brew & Bean

Founded in a small garage by two coffee enthusiasts, Brew & Bean has grown into the city's favorite coffee destination. Our passion for perfect coffee drives everything we do.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms and roast them in-house to ensure the freshest flavor in every cup.
